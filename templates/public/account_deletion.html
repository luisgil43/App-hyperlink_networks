Account and Data Deletion Request

Planix Hyperlink account and data deletion information.

Planix Hyperlink users can request account deactivation, account deletion, or deletion of personal data by contacting our support team.

How to request deletion

To request deletion of your account or personal data, please send an email to:

luisggil01@gmail.com

Use the subject: Planix Hyperlink - Account/Data Deletion Request

Please include the email address associated with your account and your full name so we can identify the account and process the request.

What data may be deleted or anonymized

Depending on your account, role, and the records associated with your use of the platform, we may delete or anonymize personal profile information, contact information, and other personal data that is no longer required for the operation of the platform.

Data that may be retained

Some operational records may be retained when required for business operations, audit history, billing, payroll, accounting, security, legal compliance, contractual obligations, dispute resolution, or fraud prevention.

Examples of records that may be retained include:

  • Production records
  • Billing records
  • Expense reports
  • Approval history
  • Audit logs
  • Payment-related records
  • Records linked to company operations

Processing time

We will review deletion requests and respond within a reasonable period. If certain data must be retained, we will explain what data is retained and why.
